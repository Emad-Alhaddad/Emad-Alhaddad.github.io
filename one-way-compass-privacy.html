Privacy Policy

One Way Compass
Last updated: January 23, 2026

Thank you for choosing One Way Compass ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Qibla direction finder application.

Summary: One Way Compass does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All processing happens locally on your device.

Information We Access

To provide accurate Qibla direction, the App accesses certain device features. Here's what we use and why:

📍 Location (GPS)

Purpose: To calculate the precise direction of the Qibla (Kaaba in Mecca) from your current position, and to provide accurate prayer times for your location.

Data handling: Your location is processed entirely on your device. It is never transmitted to any server, stored, or shared with anyone.

📷 Camera

Purpose: To enable the Augmented Reality (AR) mode that overlays the Qibla direction on your camera view for easier navigation.

Data handling: Camera access is used only for real-time display. No images or videos are captured, stored, or transmitted.

🧭 Sensors (Compass/Orientation)

Purpose: To determine which direction your device is facing and display accurate compass readings.

Data handling: Sensor data is processed in real-time on your device and is not stored or shared.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your location data
  • We do not collect device identifiers
  • We do not collect usage statistics
  • We do not use analytics services
  • We do not use advertising SDKs
  • We do not track your activity

Data Storage

The App may store minimal preferences (such as your preferred calculation method for prayer times) locally on your device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the App or clearing app data

Third-Party Services

One Way Compass does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. We do not use:

  • Analytics platforms (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics)
  • Advertising networks (e.g., AdMob, Facebook Ads)
  • Crash reporting services
  • Social media SDKs

Offline Functionality

Once your location is determined, the App can function without an internet connection. The Qibla calculation is performed locally using mathematical formulas, requiring no external servers.

Children's Privacy

One Way Compass is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, the App complies with children's privacy regulations including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of your data being compromised through our App. All processing occurs locally on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You can revoke location and camera permissions at any time through your device settings.
